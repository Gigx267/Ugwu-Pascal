Artist Statement

Paschal Ugwu Specializing in contemporary figurative expressionism with intentional and spontaneous techniques, resulting in emotionally charged figures

Statement

Telling contemporary life stories,
delving into the way people experience and interact amidst social, political, and economic issues in today’s society, especially as a Nigerian. These narratives are grounded in personal experiences, observations, and thought processes.I paint unconventional figures, highlighting emotions through their exaggerated eyes to immerse viewers in the characters’ feelings. My creative practice encompasses both Drawing and Painting, and my artistic process involves pastel or charcoal scratches, paint drips, palette knife marks, and brush strokes, shaping a contemporary figurative expressionism style.

Ugwu's artwork delves into personal experiences, human emotions, societal issues, and introspection on the Nigerian human condition
— Rele Gallery
